# SM Technology Task — FINTRIXX Landing Page

A fully responsive landing page for **FINTRIXX**, an Amazon growth & management agency.

🔗 **Live Site:** [Click Here to View](https://sm-technology-task-01.netlify.app/)

---

## 📌 About

FINTRIXX is a landing page designed to showcase Amazon seller services including PPC advertising, SEO & product ranking, inventory management, and business scaling. The page is fully responsive across all devices — mobile, tablet, and desktop.

---

## 🛠️ Built With

- HTML5
- Tailwind CSS (CDN v4)
- Vanilla CSS

---

## 📄 Sections

- Navbar & Hero Banner
- Amazon Market Statistics
- Our Story
- Why Choose Us
- Our Services
- Our Approach
- Growth Business (Harsh Realities)
- How We Help You
- Contact / Book a Call
- Footer

## 📱 Responsive

Fully responsive for all screen sizes:
- Mobile (< 640px)
- Tablet (640px – 1024px)
- Desktop (> 1024px)
